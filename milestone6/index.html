Trader's International
The Game of Global Commerce
Build you economic empire from the ground up as one of the six playable countries! Establish colonies, blockades, diplomatic treaties, and trade routes to amass as much wealth as possible! Fun for the whole family!
What is Trader's International?
Trader's International is a board game focused on international trade during the 17th and 18th centuries. Play as one of six historical empires, each with different advantages and disadvantages. Trade resources and goods, by sea or land, to other players to gain more wealth, which allows you to build bigger fleets, establish colonies, and gain more power to influence your opponents.
